Research Experiences

GPFQ Quantization

Sept. 2021 - June 2022

I worked with Professor Rayan Saab and his Ph.D Jinjie Zhang on developing a greedy path-following algorithm that performs quantization on pre-trained ML models with provable error bounds. I implemented the algorithm in PyTorch. I conducted experiments using AWS to quantize state-of-the-art neural networks (ResNets, VGGs, etc.) to verify the effectiveness of the algorithm.

Project Lim[b]itless

Oct. 2020 - June 2022

I was an undergraduate researcher in the Lim[b]itless group at Qualcomm Insitute working building a pipeline that increase the availability of prosthesis to the amputees. I worked on using transfer learning over the DeepLab deep learning network to perform background removal on the input images.

UCSD Math Honor Project

Jan. 2021 - June 2022

I completed my honor's project under Professor Mareike Dressler. I investigated on the numerical effects that different polynomial bases have when trying to certificate the nonegativity of polynomials. My thesis can be viewed here.
